The Veteran
Experience

Born in Lahore and based in Finland since the age of eighteen, Asim Searah has spent his life chasing tone. A guitarist and vocalist forged on the global metal circuit, he brings real stage and studio experience straight to your practice room.

A sought-after session musician known from Wintersun, Epica and Ne Obliviscaris, and vocalist/lyricist for The Circle, True Cult Club and Damnation Plan, Asim teaches both in person in Helsinki and online worldwide.
