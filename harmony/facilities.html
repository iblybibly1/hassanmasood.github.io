For our riders

Rider amenities

Clubhouse & café

Warm and welcoming lounge with café serving hot drinks and light meals.

Changing rooms

Spacious male and female changing rooms with showers and secure lockers.

Tack room

Secure, climate-controlled tack rooms for boarding clients. Saddle racks and personal storage.

Horse wash

Heated wash boxes with hot and cold water, drying areas, and grooming stations.

Vet & farrier bay

Dedicated area for veterinary visits and farriery, with stocks and good lighting.

Free parking

Ample free parking with trailer and lorry spaces. Easy access from the main road.
