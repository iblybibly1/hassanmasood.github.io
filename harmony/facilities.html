Our
facilities.

Every corner of Harmony Horses has been designed with a single goal — the finest possible environment for horses and riders alike.

Arena 01

Indoor arena

Our flagship heated indoor arena offers year-round riding regardless of the Finnish weather. Competition-standard lighting and footing make it equally suited to practice and performance.

Dimensions60 × 20 metres
FootingPremium sand & rubber mix
LightingFull LED — competition standard
HeatingRadiant underfloor heating
MirrorsFull-length on long side
Spectator galleryHeated seating for 40
Sound systemProfessional audio for dressage

For our riders

Rider amenities

Clubhouse & café

Warm and welcoming lounge with café serving hot drinks, light meals, and homemade baked goods.

Changing rooms

Spacious male and female changing rooms with heated showers and secure lockers.

Tack rooms

Secure, climate-controlled tack rooms for boarding clients — padded saddle racks and personal storage.

Wash boxes

Heated wash boxes with hot and cold water, infrared drying lamps, and full grooming stations.

Vet bay & parking

Dedicated veterinary treatment area and ample free parking with trailer and lorry spaces.
