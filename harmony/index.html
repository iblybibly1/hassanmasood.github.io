Equestrian Our Story

Harmony Horses was born from a desire to create the finest living conditions for HORSES — and a passion for enabling high-quality, VERSATILE and purposeful riding and training.

Harmony Horses is a premier equestrian stable in the heart of Finland's Länsi-Uusimaa region. We offer exceptional boarding, world-class coaching, and riding lessons for all ages and levels — in a warm, welcoming environment surrounded by beautiful Finnish landscape.

Our stables accommodate a select number of horses in full or tailored boarding. Riders benefit from a newly built 60×20m indoor arena, a professional all-weather outdoor school, and a 3km cross-country course through surrounding forests and fields.
