Riding lessons

Expert instruction tailored to every level — from complete beginners to advanced competitors. All ages welcome, all abilities celebrated.

  • Group lessons — maximum 6 riders per session
  • Children's beginner programmes (ages 5+)

  • Holiday riding camps and intensive weeks
  • Stable and horsemanship workshops

Horse boarding

Spacious, meticulously managed accommodation. Every horse receives personalised attention and care as if it were our own.

  • Full board: feeding, mucking out, and daily turnout included
  • Partial board with owner participation arrangements
  • 24/7 on-site stable supervision

  • Regular veterinary and farrier access
  • Monthly welfare reports sent to owners

  • Show jumping coaching — 60cm to 130cm
