Services

From your very first lesson to CHAMPIONSHIP PREPARATION — a complete equestrian experience.

01

Riding lessons

Expert instruction for every level — from complete beginners to advanced competitors. All ages welcome, all abilities celebrated.

  • Private 1-on-1 with certified instructors
  • Group lessons — maximum 6 riders
  • Children's programme (ages 5+)
  • Adult beginner and improver courses
  • Holiday camps and intensive weeks
  • Horsemanship and stable management workshops
From €60/ session
Book a lesson
02

Horse boarding

Spacious, meticulously managed accommodation. Every horse receives personalised care as if it were our own.

  • Full board — feeding, mucking out, turnout
  • Partial board with owner participation
  • 24/7 on-site stable supervision
  • Individual feeding and supplement plans
  • Regular vet and farrier coordination
  • Monthly welfare reports to owners
From €650/ month
Enquire about boarding
03

Training & coaching

Structured competitive programmes in dressage, show jumping, and eventing — from grassroots to elite level.

  • Dressage — Intro through Grand Prix
  • Show jumping — 60cm to 130cm
  • Cross-country and eventing preparation
  • Competition preparation and show support
  • Young horse training and backing
  • Fitness conditioning and flatwork
From €80/ session
Start training

Horse sales & loans

Whether buying, selling, or loaning, our experienced team guides you to the perfect match — every step of the way.

  • Curated selection of horses for sale
  • Loan horse arrangements and trials
  • Pre-purchase inspection coordination
  • Expert matching to rider ability and goals
  • Post-purchase settling-in support
