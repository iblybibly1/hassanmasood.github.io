Bridges built from real crossings

I grew up in Pakistan, studied in Finland, did an exchange semester in Turkey, and built a cross-border supply chain sourcing from China and UAE before I turned 23. International business isn't a consulting framework for me — it's how I've always operated.

I've managed EU customs paperwork, vetted international suppliers, navigated South Asian market dynamics, and built the compliance infrastructure that lets a Finnish company import from six countries without regulatory surprises. I know the practical details most market entry reports skip.

What I cover

01
Market entry research
Regulatory landscape, competitor analysis, pricing benchmarks, customer profile research, and risk-adjusted entry strategy — whether you're going into the EU, Nordics, or South Asian markets.
02
Supplier vetting & procurement
Supplier identification, due diligence frameworks, negotiation support, contract terms review, and quality control process design. Built from experience running real procurement chains.
03
EU import compliance
Customs documentation, tariff classification, CBAM obligations for goods entering the EU, intrastat reporting, and liaison with customs authorities when things get complicated.

Website copy, marketing materials, and customer communications adapted for your target market — in English, Finnish, Swedish, Arabic, Turkish, or Urdu, without translation overhead.
