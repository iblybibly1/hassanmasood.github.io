Why this is different

I implemented CBAM from scratch at FinSell Oy — reclassifying CN codes, building the supplier data pipeline, and submitting the first quarterly reports on time with zero regulatory penalties. I'm not advising from theory. I've done the work.

Most ESG consultants hand you a report. I build the system that makes compliance repeatable and manageable by your own team after I'm done — dashboards, templates, documented processes, and the training to use them.

What I cover

01
CBAM compliance
Full Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism implementation — CN-code reclassification, embedded carbon calculations, supplier emission data collection, and quarterly report preparation and submission.
02
CSRD readiness
Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive gap analysis, double materiality assessment, disclosure framework design, and first-report preparation for companies entering scope.
03
Supplier ESG data
Design and deploy supplier questionnaires, scoring frameworks, and remediation tracking. Build the data collection pipeline that feeds your CBAM and CSRD reports.
04
Reporting & dashboards
Power BI dashboards tracking compliance deadlines, emission data per shipment, and supplier scoring — turning a quarterly scramble into a continuous, manageable process.

Available in six languages

ESG consulting and all client communication available in English, Finnish, Urdu, Punjabi, Arabic, or Swedish — including supplier outreach in your suppliers' preferred language.
