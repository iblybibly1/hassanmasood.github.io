Words that travel

Most brand copy is written for one language and one market. If you're operating across borders — or want to — that's a problem. I write copy that works in English and can be adapted (not just translated) into Finnish, Urdu, Punjabi, Arabic, and Swedish without losing its edge.

I've written marketing copy, product descriptions, pitch decks, and email sequences for B2B and consumer audiences. The goal is always the same: clear, credible writing that sounds like a real person and moves the reader toward an action.

What I deliver

01
Brand voice & messaging
Tone-of-voice guide, key messages, positioning statement, and a do/don't example set that gives your team a repeatable reference for everything they write.
02
Website copy
Full-site copy structured for conversion — home, about, services, and contact pages written with SEO structure and a clear hierarchy from headline to CTA.
03
Email campaigns
Welcome sequences, nurture flows, promotional campaigns — written to be opened, read, and acted on. Structured around your funnel stage and audience temperature.
04
Pitch decks
Investor presentations and sales decks — structured argument, clear narrative, tight slides. I write the content and can advise on layout; you control the final design.

Available in six languages

All brand and content work natively available in English, Finnish, Urdu, Punjabi, Arabic, or Swedish — adapted for cultural context, not machine-translated.
