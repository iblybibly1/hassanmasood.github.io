About

Who I am.

I'm a Finland-based International Business professional specialising in sustainable operations, ESG compliance, and cross-functional strategy. My work sits at the intersection of EU regulation, operational data, and commercial execution — translating complex frameworks like CBAM and CSRD into practical systems that organisations can act on.

I graduated from Tampere University of Applied Sciences (TAMK) in December 2024 with a BBA in International Business, achieving a CGPA of 4.29/5.0. During my studies I competed in national and international competitions, ran a profitable international e-commerce store, and completed exchange studies at Ondokuz Mayıs University in Turkey.

At Tammer Brands Oy, I implemented CBAM compliance from the ground up — coordinating supplier data with Finnish Customs (Tulli), reporting to the EU portal, reclassifying CN codes to reduce customs duties, and conducting Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) data collection across the supply chain. I also led cross-functional training sessions for internal teams and supplier partners on CBAM obligations.

Beyond compliance, I'm a technologist. Self-trained in financial markets since 2012, I build algorithmic trading tools and custom KPI indicators. I placed 3rd at Huawei Tech Arena Finland 2025 developing a UI/UX interface for HarmonyOS using Vanilla JS and WebGL — the only non-CS competitor on the podium.

I hold a Finnish driving licence (A & B), am fluent in six languages, and am based in Inkoo with full mobility across Uusimaa. Actively seeking roles in sustainability, compliance, operations, and tech at €3,500–4,000/month.
